Happy Birthday

a letter written just for you

♡

Twenty-two years ago, the world quietly received one of its most extraordinary gifts — and most people had no idea. But the universe did. The stars did. And somewhere, in the soft hum of everything, so did I.

♡ · · · ♡

I have watched you laugh at your own jokes before the punchline arrives, the way your eyes crinkle first, giving it all away — and I have never wanted to look anywhere else. Your laugh is my favourite sound in every room.

·

There is a specific kind of magic in the way you move through this world — the quiet thoughtfulness you carry, the warmth you leave behind in every conversation, the way you notice the small beautiful things that most people walk past. You have always seen the world in a frequency I am still learning to hear.

♡ · · · ♡

On the days you doubt yourself — and I know those days come, even for people as remarkable as you — I hope you hear this: you are not too much, and you are not too little. You are precisely, wonderfully, exactly enough. You have always been.

♡

I think about the version of me that existed before knowing you, and I feel only a gentle sort of sympathy for that person. Because they had not yet learned what it is to have someone in your corner who genuinely, completely, deeply believes in you. You have changed the shape of my days.

· ♡ · ♡ · ♡ ·

So here is my wish for your twenty-second year: that it holds more laughter than you expect, more softness than you anticipate, and at least one moment so beautiful it stops time for a breath — the way you have stopped mine, more than once, without even trying.

·

Happy Birthday, my love. Twenty-two looks extraordinary on you.
But then again, everything does.

♡ · · · ♡

With all of my heart
